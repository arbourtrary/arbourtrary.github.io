Minimalist and patient,
Open and vacant,
Each meeting is a product unto itself

A proper
Experience 
With proper
Engagement.

Mind the details.

There is an ordered set
Of implications
We will enumerate, rank and defuse.
We choose this because
There are scripted measures
Of procession.

Part ritual, part escape,
We forsake Malcolm and Theresa.
We absolve ourselves of risky
Interaction.
